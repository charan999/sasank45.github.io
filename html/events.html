Rules for solo and group

    Solo Dance

  1. Time allotted for each participants performance is 3 minutes.
  2. The participants will be judged on the steps, expression, costume, song selection and their appeal to the audience.
  3. All kinds of dances are permitted (Folk, Western, Classical).
  4. The participants should bring the audio track in mp3 format and submit in the venue allotted at least half an hour prior to the start of the event.

    Adapt Tune

  1. Music Track will be played.
  2. The participants will be judged based on how they adapt to different styles of music.
  3. Time duration 2 minutes.
