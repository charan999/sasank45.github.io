Cultural Fest 2017

The cultural fest of 2017 by Sathyabama was a massive celebration. Culturals was conducted on 17th August 2017 with students of Sathyabama decorating the venue brightly, with their happiness and excitement. The celebration commenced and included several contests that acted as a medium for the students to showcase their talent and also win attractive prizes. The Audience were delighted when the guests Ms. Samantha, Actress and Mr.Vijay Sethupathi, Actor South Indian film industry stood among them. The students had an amazing time in the cultural fest 2017.

Cultural Fest 2018

The cultural fest of Sathyabama 2018 was a great day of celebration with bright colours everywhere. Sathyabama Culturals was conducted on 16th and 17th February 2018. This celebration brought out the hidden talents within the youngsters of Sathyabama. Several competitions were conducted and the winners of every contest were awarded with cash prizes. The students were highly energetic throughout the celebration, and the exuberance of this cultural fest was at its peak, when the event was graced by the presence of Ms.Keerthi Suresh, Actress and Mr.Siva Karthikeyan, Actor South Indian film industry on 16th February and the entire crowd was treated by Mr. Rahul Nambiar and Ms. Sharanya Srinivas troop's Musical Night. On 17th February the event was happened in presence of Shruthi Hassan, Actress South Indian Film industry and Santhosh Narayan Music Director, South Indian Film Industry.
