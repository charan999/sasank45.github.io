General Instructions

  • Prelims will be conducted between 12.00 noon to 3.00 pm from 4th February 2019 onwards.
  • Every participant is requested to register online for the corresponding event using Google Form.
  • For group events only one registration is required for a team.
  • Registration commences from 3rd January 2019 and closes by 1st February 2019.
  • Necessary materials and instruments have to be brought by the participants for all the events
  • Decision of the judge is final for all the events.
  • Participation in all events is restricted only for Sathyabama students except Final Years.
  • No act shall contain any offensive, obscene, disrespectful actions or gestures. The act will be immediately stopped and the entry will be disqualified, if these instructions are not followed.
